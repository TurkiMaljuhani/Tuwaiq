How To Predicting Used Car Prices?¶

Determining whether the listed price of a used car is a challenging task, for many factors that drive a used vehicles price on the market. The focus of this project is developing machine learning models that can accurately predict the price of a used car based on its features

Tools¶

There are tools that will be used to achieve the goal of this study, such as: matplotlib, pandas, for discovering the data and train a model. The work will be done through Jupyter notebook.
